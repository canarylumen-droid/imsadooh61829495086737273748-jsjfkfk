# Brand PDF Structure Guide for audnixai.com

To ensure audnixai.com perfectly understands your agency/business, please structure your Brand PDF (or text document) using the following sections. The AI is trained to look for these specific headers and content blocks.

## 1. Company Overview
**Header:** `COMPANY PROFILE` or `ABOUT US`
*   **Business Name:** Your official agency or business name.
*   **Mission Statement:** One sentence describing what you do and who you help.
*   **Website:** Your main URL.

## 2. Core Offer
**Header:** `OFFER` or `SERVICES`
*   **Main Value Proposition:** What specific result do you deliver? (e.g., "We help dental practices add $50k/mo in revenue").
*   **Key Deliverables:** List 3-5 main things clients get (e.g., "Meta Ads Management," "content creation," "Lead Nurturing").
*   **Pricing/Packages (Optional):** If you want the AI to know your pricing tiers.

## 3. Ideal Client Profile (ICP)
**Header:** `TARGET AUDIENCE` or `IDEAL CLIENT`
*   **Industry/Niche:** Who are you targeting? (e.g., "Real Estate Agents," "Saas Founders").
*   **Pain Points:** List 3 major problems they face that you solve.
*   **Desires:** What is their dream outcome?

## 4. Brand Voice & Tone
**Header:** `VOICE` or `TONE`
*   **Tone:** Describe how you want to sound (e.g., "Professional but friendly," "Direct and authoritative," "Casual and witty").
*   **Do's and Don'ts:**
    *   *Do:* Use emojis sparingly.
    *   *Don't:* Use corporate jargon like "synergy."

## 5. Case Studies / Social Proof
**Header:** `RESULTS` or `CASE STUDIES`
*   **Example Results:** "Client X got 20 booked calls in 30 days."
*   **Testimonials:** Key quotes from happy clients.

---

### Pro Tips for Best Results
*   **PDF Format:** Ensure the text is selectable (not a scanned image).
*   **Clarity:** Use bullet points where possible.
*   **Length:** A 2-5 page document is ideal. Too short = not enough context; Too long (20+ pages) = may dilute the focus.
